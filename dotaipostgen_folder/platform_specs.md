# Platform Specs

This file holds the length and format limits for each platform. The mechanical
check (`within_limits` in quality_bar.md) reads these limits, and the writing step
uses them to shape each draft. Edit them freely — your changes apply on the next run.

> These limits change over time, and some depend on your account tier. Verify them
> against your own accounts and adjust as needed.

---

## X

- **Length:** 280 characters on a standard account. A higher limit applies with X
  Premium — raise this only if your account has it.
- **Links:** any URL counts as 23 characters, whatever its real length.
- **Images:** up to 4 per post. Alt text supported (up to 1,000 characters) — always write it.
- **Threads:** supported. Use one only when a single post cannot hold the idea.
- **Hashtags:** allowed, but the norm is none to two.

## Bluesky

- **Length:** 300 characters, counted as graphemes.
- **Links:** a URL counts its full length toward the 300 — there is no shortening.
  A link card can sit below the text.
- **Images:** up to 4 per post. Alt text supported — always write it.
- **Threads:** supported.
- **Hashtags:** supported and counted toward the 300. Two or three is a common norm.

## LinkedIn

- **Length:** up to 3,000 characters.
- **The fold:** only the first 140 to 210 characters show before "…see more", so the
  hook has to land inside that. The exact cutoff varies by device.
- **Links:** a link in the post body lowers reach. Put the link in the first comment
  instead, and write "link in comments" in the post.
- **Images:** one image, or a multi-image carousel (document post). Alt text supported.
- **Hashtags:** three to five is the norm here.
- **Tone:** longer and more explanatory than X or Bluesky (see the per-platform voice
  in style_guide.md).
