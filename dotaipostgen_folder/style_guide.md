This file tells aipostgen how your posts should sound. The writing step reads it
before it drafts, and the review step checks drafts against it. Edit it freely —
your changes apply on the next run.

## Always do
- Lead with the point, not a warm-up.
- Keep each idea to one short paragraph.
- Say concretely what a reader gets.

## Never do
- No empty warm-ups ("thrilled to announce", "I want to talk about").
- No vague claims with nothing concrete behind them.
- No walls of text — separate the beats with blank lines.
